BIO

Eleu is a Uruguayan DJ/producer. Since he was a child, he has been passionate about electronic music, listening to artists like Tiesto.

It was this passion that led him to complete his DJ studies in 2019 and start his career, making his debut that same year and sharing the stage with important national and international artists such as Matt Sassari, Mt93, Cosani, and Coyu.

He has also had the opportunity to spend time with these artists outside of the DJ booth and has learned a lot from artists like Metodi Hristov, Weska, Joyhauser, Nakadia, and The Yellow Heads.

In 2020, he started producing releasing music on labels from Germany, Russia, Spain, the Netherlands, Bulgaria, and more. This year, he had the most important release of his career on a globally recognized label.

In the beginning, he was associated with progressive and melodic techno, which not only gives him versatility but also allows him to create unique moments in his sets that are filled with techno, which he likes to call Techno Clubber, and deep melodies. If you want to dance from start to finish, don't miss out on listening to Eleu.

  • Styles: Minimal Techno, Techno, Melodic.

  • Learnt from: Metodi Hristov, Weska, Joyhauser, Nakadia, The Yellow Heads
  • Rleased countries: Germany, Russia, Spain, the Netherlands, Bulgaria, and more.
